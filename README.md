## Project Objective
The owner of a retail store in London wants to attract more female customers the ratio of male and female is 68:32, the owner want you to suggest strategies based on the data of female customers.

## Project Insights
- Customers aged 18–59 are a high-value with two popular categories including Clothing (45%) and Accessories (31%) that have strong appeal segments for female customers.
- Top 5 items showing Blouse, Shirt, Sandals, Socks, Handbag are female-oriented segments for fashion preference.
- The distribution of sales volumn across Seasons is quite balance, suitable for campaigns and discount for all seasons.
- PayPal is the most used, following is credit card and cash. Besides, top 3 shipping types are free shipping, standard and 2-day shipping. Female customers prefer secure and familiar payment methods with cost-savings shipping types.
- Female customers are sensitive with price rather than speed, prefer cost-saving incentives.

## Final Strategy
- Invest in campaigns and banners featuring Blouse, Shirt, Sandals, Socks, Handbag with female-centric messaging like comfort, elegance and affordable.
- Sandals is kind of accessory that usually use in Summer, focus on discount, promotional campaign for this item in Summer. 
- Offer free shipping and discount campaigns targeted to female segments, especially during Spring, Fall and Winter.
- Promote 5-10% discount on bundled products purchases during sale period and promotional campaign. 
- Highlight paypal, credit card and cash in checkout payment methods on website and app.
