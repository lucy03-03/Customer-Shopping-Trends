## Project Insights
- Customers aged 18–59 are a high-value with two popular categories including Clothing (1,737 purchases) and Accessories (1,240 purchases) that have strong appeal segments for female customers.
- Top 5 items showing Blouse, Pants, Jewelry, Dress, Shirt are female-oriented segments.
- PayPal is the most used, following is credit card and cash. Besides, top 3 shipping types are free shipping, standard and store pickup. Customers prefer secure and familiar payment methods with cost-savings shipping types.
- Customers are sensitive with price rather than speed, prefer cost-saving incentives.
- Spring (999) and Fall (975) have higher purchase counts, these two seasons are periods to launch campaigns and sale-off to reach more customers.
## Final Strategy
- Invest in campaigns and banners featuring dresses, blouses, shoes, jewelry with  female-centric messaging like comfort, elegance and affordable.
- Offer free shipping and sale-off campaigns targeted to female segments, especially during Spring and Fall.
- Promote 5-10% discount on bundled products purchases during sale period and promotional campaign. 
- Highlight paypal, credit card and cash in checkout payment methods on website and app.
