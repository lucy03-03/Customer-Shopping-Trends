## Project Insights
- Clothing (1,737 purchases) and Accessories (1,240 purchases) are categories that have strong appeal segments for female customers.
- Female customers aged 32–59 are a high-value with popular items include Shoes, Coat, Blouse, Dress that are female-oriented accessories.
- Female customers are sensitive with price rather than speed, prefer cost-saving incentives.
- PayPal is the most used, following is credit card and cash. Customers prefers secure and familiar payment methods
- Spring (999) and Fall (975) have higher purchase counts, these two seasons are periods to launch campaigns with sale to reach more customers.
## Final Strategy
- Invest in campaigns and banners featuring dresses, blouses, shoes, jewelry with  female-centric messaging like comfort, elegance and affordable.
- Offer free shipping campaigns targeted to female segments, especially during Spring and Fall.
- Highlight paypal, credit card and cash in visible check out methods on website and app.
