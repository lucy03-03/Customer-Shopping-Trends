# Customer Shopping Trends Analysis (Dashboard creation using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
The retail store wants to attract more female customers, the owner want you to use the data the store has on female customers and suggest strategy for her.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/lucy03-03/Customer-Shopping-Trends/blob/main/Customer%20Shopping%20Trends.csv">Dataset</a>

## Questions
- Which category had most sales?
- Which items had most sales?
- Which payment methods that customers prefer?
- Which seasons did customers purchase most?
- Which shipping type did customer prefer?
- Which range of age did purchase most? And what category did they purchase most?

## Project Insights
- Clothing (1,737 purchases) and Accessories (1,240 purchases) are categories that have traditionally strong appeal segments for female customers.
- Female customers aged 32–59 are a high-value with popular items include Shoes, Coat, Blouse, Dress that are female-oriented accessories.
- Female customers are sensitive with price rather than speed, prefer cost-saving incentives.
- PayPal is the most used, following is credit card, customers prefers secure and familiar payment methods
- Spring (999) and Fall (975) have higher purchase counts, periods to target campaigns
## Final Strategy
- Invest in campaigns and banners featuring dresses, blouses, shoes, jewelry with  female-centric messaging like comfort and elegance
- Offer free shipping campaigns targeted to female segments, especially during Spring and Fall
- Highlight paypal and credit card in payment methods
